How to connect

I use Zoom - video conferencing platform - for my online classes.

I will send my “Zoom room” link to you via email after your registration.

You can use computer, tablet or mobile phone. Online yoga works best when you’re using a laptop with a camera at the top. This allows the camera to be adjusted so I can see everything you’re doing.

Links to download app:
(MacOS, Windows, iOS/iPadOS, Android).

You do NOT need a paid version of the app.

To connect to the class, click on the link I've sent you.

Alternatively, start the app, select "Join",
then fill in the meeting ID number and password from the email,
after that choose "Join With Video"
and subsequently "Join With Computer Audio"
